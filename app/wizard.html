Welcome.

This is a one-time setup. The app needs to know where your No Man's Sky save folder lives, where the game is installed (so it can extract item icons), and where to find hgpaktool.exe to read the game's .pak archives.

Most of this is auto-detected. You'll click through a few confirmations and then the app launches.

USE AT YOUR OWN RISK This tool only supports the latest No Man's Sky release. No back-testing. Always keep a backup of your save before applying changes — the app auto-backs up every save you load, but a manual copy you control is still wise.

Save folder.

Where No Man's Sky stores your saves. The app reads from here when you click OPEN SAVE, and writes back here when you choose WRITE NEW SLOT or OVERWRITE ORIGINAL.

NMS install.

hgpaktool.

HelloGames packs game assets into .pak archives. hgpaktool.exe is the standard reader. We can't bundle it — it's GPL-licensed and would conflict with our MIT license — so you provide your own.

checking common locations…
Where to get hgpaktool
  • Comes bundled with AMUMSS at MODBUILDER/hgpaktool.exe — easiest if you already mod NMS.
  • Or download standalone from zencq/HGPak releases and drop it at tools/hgpaktool.exe in this repo.

Extract icons.

Reads NMSARC.TexUI.pak from your install, extracts ~1,600 DDS textures, converts each to PNG. Takes 1–2 minutes. The app works without icons (colored badges instead) — you can re-run extraction anytime from the Settings tab.
